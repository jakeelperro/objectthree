CALICALABOZO

Website

Calicalabozo is an interactive website made to represent the cultural aspects from the city and the people that the writer Andrés Caicedo has inside his books and short stories.

Art Direction · Development · Ui · UX · Motion Graphics

We recolected information about the writer and references to the city and culture inside his books.

The site is divided into two principal sections: the first section is a quick summary with relevant work and history about Andrés Caicedo that we called: Biography.

The second section contains four different experiences related to the cultural identity inside the books like music, places, objects and ways to express. We called this section Writes.
