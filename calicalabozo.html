CALICALABOZO

Website

Calicalabozo is an interactive website made to represent the cultural aspects from the city that the writer Andrés Caicedo has inside his books and short stories.

Art Direction · Development · Ui · UX · Motion Graphics

The navigation its divided in three sections and each one has an experience related to the writer and references to the city and culture inside his books.

Inspired by the stories inside the books, we created four different interactions that represents an aspect of the cultural identity in each section.

The interactive map shows the users how the city has changed with a before/after of the places - some of them don't exist any more - that are mentioned by the writer on his books and short stories.
